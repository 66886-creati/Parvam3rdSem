The One Piece

The most famous lines in One Piece are likely those
spoken by the protagonist, Monkey D. Luffy,
such as "A man's dream will never die!" or "No matter how hard or impossible it is, never lose sight of your goal," which capture the spirit of adventure and perseverance central to the series.
Other iconic lines include "Scars on the back are a swordsman's greatest shame!" from Roronoa Zoro, and "What keeps me alive in this world is neither bodily organs nor muscles. It is...my soul!" from Brook.
